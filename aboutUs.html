We are the direct supplier of Tmall Supermarket and Jingdong Supermarket in China's two largest home appliance platforms; we are familiar with the operating modes of these two platforms and the good relationship between buildings. Today, we have good cooperation with major e-commerce platforms in China, and we have completed nearly 90% of e-commerce platform deployment, with strong e-commerce operation capabilities and rich experience. At the same time, we are committed to promoting high-quality overseas brands to the Chinese market, using our network-wide e-commerce platform and powerful offline resources to enable Chinese consumers to understand our products faster and more accurately. In the future, we will focus on long-term cooperation and strategic thinking with the brand, and use our ability to understand the Chinese market to bring healthy and quality life to thousands of households in China.
